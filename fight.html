They villagers have heard rumor that programatic monsters are overflowing from the dungeon. They call upon the hero known only as The Engineer to assist them. You answer there call, and shortly after arriving at the dungeon you find a dreaded BUG!

Not to worry, you should be able to defeat it using your skills...

Each card in your deck costs 1 Energy so choose your actions carefully.

When you are done playing cards, press the END TURN button but beware! Once your turn is over the monster will attack!

Make sure you have some BLOCK to protect yourself.

Fear Not! When your DRAW PILE runs out, your DISCARD PILE will be shuffled back in to replace it.
